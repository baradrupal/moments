We are Moments Events & Entertainment

Moments Event and Entertainment is a leading event management company based in Gujarat. We specialize in Wedding Planning, Corporate Events, Exhibitions, & Religious Functions. At Moments, we work hard and invest all of our energy to make your dreams into realities. Our team interacts with clients, understand their exact needs and do the flawless execution of the commitment. Moments has the necessary experience and expertise to get you the best and most suitable customized services for your corporate and personal requirements. Our passion is to make the entire event stress free, enjoyable and just perfect for your life's celebrations. And take this word from us: We always go beyond the clientele's expectations. We constitute the choicest source for customer's requirement to serve them with the best and bring smile on their face.

Moments began its journey from July, 2008 and has made a mark in the field of event management in a very short time. Moments is the brain child of Mr. Hirav Shah; a technocrat with a vision of giving the best services in the event and entertainment industry.

Why Moments?

We, at Moments, just take all the responsibilities from your shoulders and work for you from A to Z. We plan, we assist, we create, we coordinate, we modify, we design, we do it all for you; the way you want and make your event a happening one.

Our team is always inclined towards making the best of events. We specialize in strategizing systematically and developing a customized approach for all the events. This makes us stand out. We take up every event as own and that is our USP. The MD has a personal visit at the events to bring momentum to the work and support the staff to make your event the best. We have the latest infrastructure and updated ideas for the events. For us, all clients are equal. We work equally hard for any event regardless of budget.
